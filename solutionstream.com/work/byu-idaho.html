BYU Idaho

The Brief

Brigham Young University-Idaho is a private, four-year university. BYU-Idaho seeks to create a wholesome learning environment in which students can receive a quality education.

BYU-Idaho students needed a reliable self-help system that gave them the academic information and advice to help them choose a major, plan courses within their major, improve academic performance, and succeed after graduation.

The Solution

SolutionStream Business Analysts collaborated with BYU-Idaho to define the business rules, scope, and strategy of multiple new systems. The SolutionStream design team created a modern design for the Online Scheduling Management (OMS) and the Educational Position System (EPS). SolutionStream developers and project managers helped BYU-Idaho helped train the BYU Idaho development and design teams in development best practices.

SolutionStream worked with BYU-Idaho to create a system that guides & supports students through their academic life cycle.

The Impact

SolutionStream worked with BYU-Idaho to create a system that guides & supports students through their academic life cycle. Using embedded SolutionStream teams allowed BYU Idaho to learn more efficient processes and deliver the redesigned system on time and on budget.

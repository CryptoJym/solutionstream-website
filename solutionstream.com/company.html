Our Story

We've Been Doing This Since 1997

Almost three decades of building software for companies that needed platforms to scale, systems to modernize, or teams that could deliver.

We're based in Lehi, Utah. Our engineers are full-time employees who embed with your team. We use AI tools when they make sense, proven methodologies always.

  • Young Living: $200M → $2B revenue growth on platforms we built.
  • SeneGence: 50K → 500K distributors on infrastructure designed to scale.
  • Modere: 13 markets launched in 3 months with our help.

We're not the flashiest agency. We're the one that sticks around.

Our Culture

SolutionStream knows how to have fun, whether at work or not, and we show it with events like family theme park days, BBQs, cake days, company movies, and gift parties.

All SolutionStream consultants are full-time employees with great benefits, 401k matching, and hourly pay, meaning if you work extra, we pay extra.

We do it because we like building great software—and, sure, the paycheck is nice.
